Fun,Thrill & Excitement for Everyone...Guaranteed!!!


Discover an out of the world fun, excitement, adventure, and enchanting environment at Black Thunder, a theme park near Ooty - Asia’s No.1 water Theme Park. Nestled in the foothills of the scenic Nilgiri Hills, this ultimate holiday destination offers you a variety of fabulous water rides, mind-boggling dry rides, boating, mud karting, leisure hotel, a resort and much more. To describe this Water Theme Park, Coimbatore in its entirety which stands by its byline “Great Place and Great Fun” is almost impossible. Black Thunder is not just another water theme park, it is the grandest, greatest of them all. With a backdrop of the Nilgiris, Black Thunder Water Theme Park in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, is a place of unlimited fun and adventure. On offer are a combination of land games and water games in the largest wave pool in India. Be ready to experience exotic camel rides, poolside frolic, video games and a ride in the 1.8 Kilometre long moat that surrounds the park.
