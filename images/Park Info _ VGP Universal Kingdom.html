Safety Tips

Better safe than sorry! And so while our Kutti Raja wants you to wring ever drop of fun out of your day at our little kingdom, he insists that you follow a few rules to ensure your safety. Here they are!

Please secure all loose articles such as wallets, change, sunglasses, cell phones and hats.

Please use the safety equipment provided properly; do not attempt to loosen or wriggle free of restraints or safety devices.

Please remain seated until you are instructed to exit and ensure that your children understand and adhere to safety practices on the rides.

Please report any unsafe behaviour or condition on a ride to a supervisor or manager at once.

Maintenance

Your safety and security is a constant priority at our little kingdom. We make certain that you stay safe with a rigorous maintenance schedule to ensure that every ride and attraction is in ship-shape, and to derail any potential threats to your safety well in advance. Needless to say, our trained team of technicians are vigilant in discharging the mammoth responsibility that they have been tasked with – and our safety record speaks volumes of their commitment and efficiency.
