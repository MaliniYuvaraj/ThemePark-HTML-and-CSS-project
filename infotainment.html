Infotainment at Black Thunder Theme Park in Coimbatore

Aquarium

The Aquarium at Black Thunder, our water park in Coimbatore, is constructed over an area of 1500. Sq. Ft. has over 100 extraordinary varieties of fishes. Some of the exotic fishes are Rainbow Shark, Black Moors, Cardinal Tetra and so on.

Bird Park

The excited twittering of birds and the colourful flapping of a hundred wings welcome you to Black Thunder’s Birds Park. At our amusement park in Coimbatore, take a relaxing and informative tour – each species is tagged with a personal name card and other interesting details. A treat for the eyes and mind.

Rain Forest

Black Thunder, our amusement park in Coimbatore, is encompassed with a variety of plantations and trees. There are some rare plants and trees in the park, which are being safeguarded for posterity.
